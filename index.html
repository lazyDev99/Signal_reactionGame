SIGNAL
1v1 Reaction Speed Duel

What is this?

SIGNAL is a two-player reaction time game played on one phone. It measures choice reaction time — how fast your brain can identify a stimulus and select the correct response. This is a validated cognitive science measure of processing speed.

Unlike simple reflex games, SIGNAL uses categorization tasks (colours, directions, numbers, animals) that require genuine cognitive processing, not just twitchy fingers.

How to play

  • Place the phone between two players. Player 1 sits at the top (phone rotated), Player 2 at the bottom.
  • Each round, a stimulus appears on both sides of the screen.
  • Tap the correct answer from the buttons below your stimulus — as fast as possible.
  • Both players answer simultaneously. The round ends when both answer correctly or time runs out.

Scoring

  • Correct answer — your reaction time for that round is saved.
  • First wrong tap — +500ms penalty added to your score.
  • Second or third wrong tap — +100ms each.
  • No answer before time runs out — the full round duration is added. Very punishing.
  • Winner = lowest average reaction time across all rounds.

Task types

  • Arrow direction — tap which way the arrow points.
  • Odd or even — categorize the number shown.
  • Shape colour — tap the colour of the shape, not the word written on it.
  • Larger number — pick the bigger of two numbers.
  • Land or sea — categorize the animal shown.
